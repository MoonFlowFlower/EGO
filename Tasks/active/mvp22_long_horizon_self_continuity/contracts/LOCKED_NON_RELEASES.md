# WP17 / MVP22 Locked Non-Releases

以下能力在 `WP17/MVP22` authority freeze 阶段明确 **不放开**：

- live autonomy
- OpenEmotion direct reply authority
- tool execution authority
- broader transport claims
- open-world free-form autonomy
- direct mutation of `WP8~WP16` owner state
- authority escalation bypass

即使后续 `WP17` 拿到 controlled `E5`，这些 non-releases 也不会自动解锁；必须依赖新的 authority package。
