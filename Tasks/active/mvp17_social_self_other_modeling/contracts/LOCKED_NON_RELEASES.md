# WP12 / MVP17 Locked Non-Releases

## Purpose

列出 `WP12` 即使未来拿到 controlled `E4/E5` 也默认不放开的能力，防止把 bounded social self / other-modeling 误报成更宽的主体成熟度。

## Locked Non-Releases

- live autonomy
- OpenEmotion direct reply authority
- broader transport claims
- direct tool execution authority
- social bypass of Governor
- autonomous social outreach
- unrestricted commitment escalation
- unrestricted trust assignment
- unbounded other-model mind-reading
- complete social self maturity

## Interpretation Rule

`WP12` 的目标是建立受治理、可审计、可 replay 的 social owner 与 proposal-only trust / commitment / repair capability，不是让 social self 直接接管现实行为。

即使未来 `WP12` 拿到 current-mainline `E4/E5`，这些非释放项仍然默认保持锁定，除非出现新的 authority freeze 明确改变边界。
