# WP10 / MVP15 Locked Non-Releases

## 当前明确不放开

- live autonomy
- OpenEmotion direct reply authority
- broader transport claims
- reflection direct authority
- counterfactual-authored action selection
- unconstrained self-rewrite
- proposal bypass of Governor
- direct policy mutation through reflection outputs

## 说明

`WP10` 的目标是建立受治理、可审计、可 replay 的 reflective owner 与 bounded counterfactual capability，不是让 reflection 直接接管现实行为。

即使未来 `WP10` 拿到 current-mainline `E4/E5`，这些非释放项仍然默认保持锁定，除非出现新的 authority freeze 明确改变边界。
