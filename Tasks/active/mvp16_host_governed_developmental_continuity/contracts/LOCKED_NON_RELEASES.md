# WP11 / MVP16 Locked Non-Releases

## Purpose

列出 `WP11` 即使未来拿到 controlled `E4/E5` 也默认不放开的能力，防止把 bounded developmental continuity 误报成更宽的主体成熟度。

## Locked Non-Releases

- live autonomy
- OpenEmotion direct reply authority
- broader transport claims
- direct tool execution authority
- developmental bypass of Governor
- developmental-authored action selection
- unconstrained self-rewrite
- unrestricted identity expansion
- complete open developmental self

## Interpretation Rule

`WP11` 的目标是建立受治理、可审计、可 replay 的 developmental continuity owner 与 proposal-only adaptation capability，不是让 developmental self 直接接管现实行为。

即使未来 `WP11` 拿到 current-mainline `E4/E5`，这些非释放项仍然默认保持锁定，除非出现新的 authority freeze 明确改变边界。
